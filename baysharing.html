Bay Sharing

Previous Next

Bay Sharing is a single page application providing a platform where the users can share their items with other people or families in the Bay Area.

Users can view all the post list, filter the posts by search term, group the posts by age, city, user, or category by clicking on the tags on each post, and check out the post details by clicking on the post card. They can save posts as favorite, add comments, send invites to the posts which they are interested in. They can also create, edit or delete their own post. It is easy to view the posts which they have sent invites to and the invites they received from other users. When the user and aother user have interest in each other's post(s), a deal is available to made. Then they can contact each other through email to discuss the furture process to share their items.
