Meal Joy

Previous Next

Meal Joy is a web application that users can explore tons of healthy recipes, create their DIY recipes and make their weekly meal plan.

Logged in users can browser popular recipe list, view the complete detail information for each recipe by clicking on the recipe image, search recipes by typing key words, filter the recipes by applying one or multiple filters, view all the food categories and check out recipes for each category. They can also save recipes as favorite and view all saved favorite recipes. By filling in the form, users can create DIY recipes and can also edit or delete them. On each recipe detail page (including DIY recipes), users can add current recipe to their weekly meal plan by clicking on the "Add to My Meal Plan" button and selecting the day of the week and the meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner or snack). Thay can view the whole weekly meal plan table and edit it at will.
